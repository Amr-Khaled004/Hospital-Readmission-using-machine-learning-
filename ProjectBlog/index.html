Introduction

In this project we aim to reduce the risk of Diabetes - Readmission within 30 days , Why?!

In early eighteenth,doctors and scientists were shocked by the amount of readmitted diabetic patients after finishing their treatment period or even worse dying before be able to readmit to hospital again due to sudden increase in glucose level.In 1860 , Scientist Samuel came with the solution by introducing the effect of A1C on diabetes , and from here doctors begin to test both glucose and A1C.After that many parameters came into stage until becoming about 50 attributes making it hard or seem impossible for the doctor to know if he should let the patient leave hospital or no.Here come our code in helping doctor saving diabetic patient money,time and most important lifes.

By using the machine learning algoriths We can implement a model to predict if a certain patient is readmitted or no.

Build & Train Model:

It is now time for us to split our data. The idea behind splitting the data is so that we can measure how well your model would do on unseen data. We spilt our data using K-fold cross validation method as This method enable us to use all data in train and test so we can improve our model performance. The principle of K-fold cross validation method is

1. split our original data into K samples

2.one of these samples is held out and iterate to the rest

3. the sample which is held out is used to make decisions on how to improve the model ( for test) and the rest is used to train

4. hold out the next one and iterate to the rest and so on till iterating on k samples
